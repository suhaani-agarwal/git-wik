# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Focus critique on ideas and code, not people.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, PRs, or contributors that violate this code.

## Reporting

Report violations privately through project maintainers or GitHub maintainers contact channels.
